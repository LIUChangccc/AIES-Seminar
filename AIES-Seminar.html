Time Keynote
March 31st
Battle against uncertainty: biological evolution and human behavior
Jin Yoshimura, PhD

Abstract: Organisms daily fight against environmental uncertainty for own survival and the reproduction of his/her offspring. This survival strategy, or in another word, extinction avoidance strategy, had prompted the evolution of organisms. As an example, I introduce the evolution of periodical cicadas: how they had evolved prime-numbered life cycles (17-year and 13-year). Similarly, the mankind had invented democracy and capitalism in order to avoid bankruptcy as a rational behavior. I reviewed the life of human (myself including) and other lives in terms of survival strategy against uncertainty of the environments.
